Space Exploration Over the Years

Discover the incredible journey of humanity's exploration of the cosmos.

1957

Launch of Sputnik 1, the first artificial Earth satellite. This marked the beginning of the space age.

1961

Yuri Gagarin becomes the first human to travel into space, orbiting Earth aboard Vostok 1.

1969

Apollo 11 mission successfully lands astronauts Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin on the Moon.

1977

Voyager 1 and 2 are launched to explore the outer solar system, providing valuable data about distant planets.

1986

Space Shuttle Challenger disaster, a tragic event that resulted in the loss of seven astronauts.

1990

Launch of the Hubble Space Telescope, which has provided stunning images of distant galaxies and celestial objects.

2004

Opportunity rover lands on Mars, beginning its mission to study the planet's surface and geology.

2012

Curiosity rover lands on Mars, conducting extensive research on the planet's climate and geology.

2015

New Horizons spacecraft flies by Pluto, capturing detailed images and data about the dwarf planet.

2020

Perseverance rover lands on Mars, aiming to search for signs of past microbial life and collect samples.

2021

James Webb Space Telescope is launched, expected to revolutionize our understanding of the universe's earliest moments.
